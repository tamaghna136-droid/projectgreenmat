How does it work?

A Green Mat consists of three layers- a layer of home-made organic fertilizer between two jute mats, which are lightly coated with a layer of starch paste to prevent water leakages. They consist of small holes to plant seeds in. After you plant the seeds, lay the mats on the soil bed and cover them up with a light layer of soil, and wait for them work their magic. The main benefit of these mats is that they can be used to plant crops in almost any type of soil, as they contain the essential ingredients required for plant growth.
